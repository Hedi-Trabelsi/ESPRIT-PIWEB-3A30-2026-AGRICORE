# 🌱 ESPRIT-PIWEB-3A30-2026-AGRICORE

## 📌 Overview
Agricore is a web application developed as part of the **PIWEB – 3rd Year Engineering Program** at **Esprit School of Engineering (Academic Year 2025–2026)**.

The goal of this project is to help farmers manage their agricultural activities efficiently through a centralized web platform.

Agricore provides modules for users, animals, equipment, events, finance, and maintenance management.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 👤 Users Management
- Manage accounts for farmers, technicians, and financial managers  
- Authentication and role-based access control  

### 🐄 Animals Management
- Track livestock  
- Monitor health status  

### 🚜 Equipment Management
- Manage agricultural tools and machinery  
- Track availability, usage, and stock levels  

### 📅 Events Management
- Create and manage agricultural events  
- Track participation  

### 💰 Finance Management
- Track expenses and revenues  
- Generate financial reports  

### 🔧 Maintenance Management
- Schedule equipment maintenance  
- Track maintenance history
